About Us

Jaddu's Food Field is a family style restaurant offering exceptional value to its guests through the wide range of Mexican, Chinese, Thai, Indian, and Continental cuisines. Indian Cricketer Ravindra Jadeja crafted Jaddu’s with a singular vision-- bringing his guests best of the best and making any meal feel like a homemade meal.

Platters of homestyle Indian curries, soups, decadent continental dishes or juicy starters, and of course homemade desserts are all staples at Jaddu’s, meant for sharing amongst friends and family. When great food and drinks are supported by friendly and knowledgeable service, the results are magic - we want every day to feel like a Sunday afternoon at home!

Jaddu's was opened in 2012 in Rajkot to provide delicious food as well as magnificient service for your particular needs. In order to achieve this, professional chefs and staff were handpicked, along with the modern kitchen facility and cozy interior. To further enhance the dining experience, each meal is prepared using the most fresh, high quality, and rich in flavor ingredients!

We hope to see you soon at Jaddu's!
